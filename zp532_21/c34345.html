2002 World Cup Cameroon Signed Kit.

The famous vest kit that was banned by FIFA before the World Cup. Cameroon used the vest, but sewed sleeves into it for the tournament games.

This shirt was glued to the backing, and glue and paper backing have stuck to the shirt. I think it will need professional care to remove this. I recommend mounting it in the frame provided.

Shirt is signed by the Cameroon squad whilst based in Kawaguchiko Town in Japan.
